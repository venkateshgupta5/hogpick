Story

22 NOV 235 Likes 3 Comments 20 share

I started playing cricket when I was in class five. 'Cricket was my life then'. I enjoyed playing cricket more than I loved doing anything else. After playing a lot of 'gully' tournaments in my hometown Begusarai; I got selected for the district Under 14 team but my father always had his own insecurities and apprehensions towards cricket. He just didn't like the fact that I played cricket.

Then one day, I heard that there was a trial scheduled ahead for the selection of the state cricket team in Patna. I feared my father but I also feared loosing cricket; so I boarded a passenger train from Begusarai to Patna. I was rejected at the trials. I didn't know why? When I was packing my bag, someone abruptly approached me from back and said 'Yahan selection aise nhi hota hai! Teen lakh do, hum tmhara selection abhi krwa denge'. (Selection in the state team doesn't happens like the way you think. Offer me 3 lakhs, I will make sure that you are in the team.

I was numb. I boarded the train back to Begusarai and reached my home at two in the night. Everyone in my family, supposed that I was at my friend's house. That was the last day, I ever took cricket seriously!

I never ever wanted to pursue engineering. I hate Maths. I just hate it. I wanted to take commerce without Mathematics and wished to dance but in the quest of an employable course, I am here doing engineering.

I know it isn't the end but it's too late to live my dream further. I'm not able to devote my entire time to dancing while a lot of people I meet taking dance seriously are doing so. I just wish that I could have joined dance classes earlier in my life but alas! I am pursuing engineering.

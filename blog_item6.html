I come from Srinagar. Yes, I come from Kashmir! Whenever I say, that I am from the valley, there are people who start raising their eyebrows the other way around and frame bigoted opinion. Kashmir isn’t the same as you see in the media. I haven’t seen a single bullet being fired in my locality in the past 15 years.

Kashmir is frigid but trust me; the people aren’t. Kashmir is enthralling and enticing. Tourists often feel that we don’t feel cold! It isn’t so. We also feel the brumal weather as much as you do.
